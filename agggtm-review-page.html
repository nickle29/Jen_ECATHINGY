A Good Girl's Guide to Murder - The Series Review

A Good Girl's Guide to Murder

I want to start this review by saying that I am a true crime and all things thriller/mystery fan, so I absolutely loved this book. At first, when I heard of this book on social media, I was thrown off by the title, but after hearing the tens of thousands of positive reviews, I caved. After finally finishing it, I can say that it's one of the best books I've read in a while. This book had me on the edge of my seat at all times reading it, whether it be from the case to what the characters would do next.
